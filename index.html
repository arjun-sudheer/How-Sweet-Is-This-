About Toblerone

Toblerone is a well-known Swiss chocolate brand that originated in Bern, Switzerland. Created by Theodor Tobler and Emil Baumann in 1908, Toblerone is recognized for its distinctive triangular shape, which symbolizes the Swiss Alps. The chocolate bar typically consists of milk chocolate blended with honey, almonds, and nougat, providing a unique and delicious taste. Over the years, Toblerone has become a globally recognized and beloved chocolate brand.
